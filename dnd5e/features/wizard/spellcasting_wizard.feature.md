# Spellcasting Wizard Feature

## Features

### Spellcasting Wizard

- kind: feature
- id: spellcasting_wizard
- source: wizard
- usage: at will
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.wizard.spellcasting

> You have a spellbook containing spells that show the first glimmerings of
> your true power. As a student of arcane magic, you have a spellbook
> containing 6 wizard spells of your choice (1st level or higher).
> Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for your wizard spells.
