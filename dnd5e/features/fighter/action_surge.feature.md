# Action Surge Feature

## Features

### Action Surge

- kind: feature
- id: action_surge
- source: fighter
- usage: 1 per short rest
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.fighter.action_surge

> On your turn you can push yourself beyond your normal limits. Take one
> additional action. Once you use this feature, you must finish a short
> or long rest before you can use it again.
