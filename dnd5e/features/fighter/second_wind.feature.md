# Second Wind Feature

## Features

### Second Wind

- kind: feature
- id: second_wind
- source: fighter
- usage: 1 per short rest
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.fighter.second_wind

> You have a limited well of stamina you can draw on to protect yourself from
> harm. On your turn you can use a bonus action to regain 1d10 + your fighter
> level hit points. Once you use this feature, you must finish a short or
> long rest before you can use it again.
