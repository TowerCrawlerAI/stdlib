# Sneak Attack Feature

## Features

### Sneak Attack

- kind: feature
- id: sneak_attack
- source: rogue
- usage: 1 per turn
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.rogue.sneak_attack

> Once per turn you can deal extra damage to one creature you hit with an
> attack if you have advantage on the attack roll, or if another enemy of
> the target is adjacent to it and you don't have disadvantage on the roll.
> The extra damage is 3d6 at 5th level (scales with rogue level).
