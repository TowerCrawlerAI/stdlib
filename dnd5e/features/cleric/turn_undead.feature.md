# Turn Undead Feature

## Features

### Turn Undead

- kind: feature
- id: turn_undead
- source: cleric
- usage: 1 per short rest (via channel_divinity)
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.cleric.turn_undead

> As an action, you present your holy symbol and speak a prayer censuring
> the undead. Each undead that can see or hear you within 30 feet must make
> a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is turned for 1
> minute or until it takes any damage.
