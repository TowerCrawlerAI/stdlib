# Channel Divinity Feature

## Features

### Channel Divinity

- kind: feature
- id: channel_divinity
- source: cleric
- usage: 1 per short rest
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.features.cleric.channel_divinity

> You can channel divine energy directly from your deity, using that energy
> to fuel magical effects. You start with two such effects: Turn Undead and
> an effect determined by your domain.
