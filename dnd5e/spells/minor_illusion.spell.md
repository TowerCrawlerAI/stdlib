# Minor Illusion Spell

## Spells

### Minor Illusion

- kind: spell
- id: minor_illusion
- level: 0
- school: illusion
- casting_time: action
- range: 30 ft
- components: [s, m]
- duration: 1 minute
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.minor_illusion

> You create a sound or an image of an object within range that lasts for the
> duration. The illusion also ends if you dismiss it as an action or if you cast
> this spell again. If you create a sound, its volume can range from a whisper
> to a shout. It can be your voice, someone else's voice, a lion's roar, a clap
> of thunder, or any other sound you choose. The sound continues uninterrupted
> throughout the duration, or you can make discrete sounds at different times
> before the spell ends.
