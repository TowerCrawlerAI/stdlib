# Vicious Mockery Spell

## Spells

### Vicious Mockery

- kind: spell
- id: vicious_mockery
- level: 0
- school: enchantment
- casting_time: action
- range: 60 ft
- components: [v]
- duration: instantaneous
- damage: "1d4"
- damage_type: psychic
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.vicious_mockery

> You unleash a string of insults laced with subtle enchantments at a creature
> you can see within range. Make a Wisdom saving throw, or the creature takes
> 1d4 psychic damage and has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes
> before the end of its next turn (damage increases at higher levels: 2d4 at 5th,
> 3d4 at 11th, 4d4 at 17th).
