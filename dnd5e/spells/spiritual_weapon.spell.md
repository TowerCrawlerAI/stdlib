# Spiritual Weapon Spell

## Spells

### Spiritual Weapon

- kind: spell
- id: spiritual_weapon
- level: 2
- school: evocation
- casting_time: bonus
- range: 60 ft
- components: [v, s]
- duration: 1 minute
- damage: "1d8"
- damage_per_upcast_slot: "1d8"
- damage_type: force
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.spiritual_weapon

> You create a floating, spectral weapon within range. When you cast the
> spell, you can make a melee spell attack against a creature within 5 ft of
> the weapon. On a hit, it takes 1d8 + your spellcasting modifier force damage.
> As a bonus action on your turn you can move the weapon up to 20 ft and
> repeat the attack.
