# Hold Person Spell

## Spells

### Hold Person

- kind: spell
- id: hold_person
- level: 2
- school: enchantment
- casting_time: action
- range: 60 ft
- components: [v, s, m]
- duration: concentration, up to 1 minute
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.hold_person

> Choose a humanoid that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a
> Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed for the duration. At the end of each of
> the target's turns, it can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the
> spell ends on that target.
