# Mage Hand Spell

## Spells

### Mage Hand

- kind: spell
- id: mage_hand
- level: 0
- school: conjuration
- casting_time: action
- range: 30 ft
- components: [v, s]
- duration: 1 minute
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.mage_hand

> A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range. The
> hand lasts for the duration or until you dismiss it. You can use the hand
> to manipulate an object, open a container, or stow/retrieve items.
