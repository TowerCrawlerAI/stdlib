# Mage Armor Spell

## Spells

### Mage Armor

- kind: spell
- id: mage_armor
- level: 1
- school: abjuration
- casting_time: action
- range: touch
- components: [v, s, m]
- duration: 8 hours
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.mage_armor

> You touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor, and a protective magical
> force surrounds it. For the duration, the target's AC is 13 + its Dexterity
> modifier. The spell ends if the target dons armor or if you dismiss the spell
> as an action.
