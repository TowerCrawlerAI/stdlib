## Spells

### Fire Bolt

- kind: spell
- id: fire_bolt
- level: 0
- school: evocation
- casting_time: action
- range: 120 ft
- components: [v, s]
- duration: instantaneous
- damage: "2d10"
- damage_type: fire
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.fire_bolt

> You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged
> spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 2d10 fire damage
> (damage increases at higher levels: 1d10 at 1st, 2d10 at 5th, 3d10 at 11th,
> 4d10 at 17th).
