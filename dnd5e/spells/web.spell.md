# Web Spell

## Spells

### Web

- kind: spell
- id: web
- level: 2
- school: conjuration
- casting_time: action
- range: 60 ft
- components: [v, s, m]
- duration: concentration, up to 1 hour
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.web

> You conjure a mass of thick, sticky webbing at a point of your choice within
> range. The webs fill a 20-foot cube from that point for the duration. The
> webs are difficult terrain, and any creature that starts its turn in the webs
> or enters them during its turn must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be
> restrained. A creature can take an action to make a Strength check against your
> spell save DC, freeing itself or another creature in the webs on a success.
> Each 10-foot cube of webs is destroyed if exposed to fire.
