# Light Spell

## Spells

### Light

- kind: spell
- id: light
- level: 0
- school: evocation
- casting_time: action
- range: 60 ft
- components: [v, m]
- duration: 1 hour
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.light

> You touch an object that is no larger than 10 feet in any dimension. The
> object shines with a bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an
> additional 20 feet. The light can be colored as you wish. Completely covering
> the object with an opaque material blocks the light. The spell ends if you
> cast it again or dismiss it as an action.
