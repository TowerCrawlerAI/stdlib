# Invisibility Spell

## Spells

### Invisibility

- kind: spell
- id: invisibility
- level: 2
- school: illusion
- casting_time: action
- range: touch
- components: [v, s, m]
- duration: concentration, up to 1 hour
- mechanics_module: tower.rules.spells.invisibility

> A creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends. Anything the
> target is wearing or carrying is invisible as long as it is on the target's
> person. The spell ends for a target that attacks or casts a spell.
